Identity Constellation

Where you come from shapes how you lead.

Activity · 15–20 minutes  |  Leadership Begins With You  |  Section 1: Your Leadership Story

Map the identities, communities, and experiences that have shaped your leadership. Enter your name below — it will appear at the center of your constellation. Then click "+ Add Identity" to name and place each circle. Drag any circle to reposition it. Lines connect related identities automatically.

Prompts to Guide You
What identities matter most to you?
What communities shaped your worldview?
What responsibilities or roles have influenced how you show up?
What experiences taught you resilience, courage, or empathy?

The Unclear Expectations

Your campus organization has unclear expectations. Members are confused about roles and responsibilities. Meetings feel unproductive. People are showing up inconsistently and the group's culture is drifting. As a newer member, you're frustrated — but unsure if you have the standing to say anything. You don't have a formal title or an official leadership role.

Reflection Questions

JOURNAL
1
Which 3–5 values feel most essential to who you are as a leader?
2
How do these values show up — or get suppressed — in different contexts (school, work, family, community)?
3
Who taught you each value? Family, community, an experience, or hard necessity?
4
Where do you feel the most alignment between your values and your actions? The most tension?
💬 Discussion Prompt

Share your top 3 values and one specific story of how you've lived one of them in action. What made that moment feel like leadership — even if no one called it that?

Take a few minutes to write — don't overthink it. These prompts are designed to help you begin connecting your story to your leadership. There are no right or wrong answers. Write what's honest and true for you. You can respond in any language(s) you think in.

1
What parts of your life story have shaped how you show up for others?

Think about experiences — difficult, joyful, or both — that changed how you see yourself or the world.

When you finish, add your key reflections to Section 1 of your Your Leadership Operating System (YLOS). Specifically: which form(s) of cultural wealth feel strongest for you, and one sentence describing what leadership means to you right now. It's okay if this changes — write what's true today.
